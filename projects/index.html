EndoMapper

Together with Universidad de Zaragoza and Université Clermont Auvergne we work on a real time mapping platform for autonomous navigation for endoscopy: EndoMapper. Before joining the project I worked on depth estimation in endoscopy. Because ground truth cannot be acquired in-vivo the main challenge was overcoming the difference in domains between synthetic data and real images.

Implicit domain adaptation with conditional generative adversarial networks for depth prediction in endoscopy.

Anita Rau, PJ Eddie Edwards, Omer F. Ahmad, Paul Riordan, Mirek Janatka, Laurence B. Lovat, and Danail Stoyanov. IJCARS, 2019.

Find the paper here . I also published our dataset here for others to use and some code for the generation of synthetic data in Unity.
