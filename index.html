About

I am a third-year PhD candidate with the Surgical Robot Vision group at University College London. My main focus is 3D reconstruction from monocular images. My research interests include machine learning techniques for depth and camera pose estimation, navigation and mapping methods, domain transfer, and representation learning.

Before joining my research group, I got a MSc in Computational Statistics and Machine Learning from UCL and a BSc in Mathematics in Economics from University of Mannheim. I recently also spent a few months at Niantic working on human interpretable image relationships.
